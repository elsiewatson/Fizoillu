What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device every time you visit a website. This enables us to understand how to interact better with our site, remember your preferences, and make your general browsing smoother.

 

Use of Cookies

Fizoillu makes use of cookies for:

  • Website Performance: In order for our website to function properly and respond accordingly.
  • User Preferences: To save user preferences, say language choice or even display preferences, etc.
  • Analytics: This is to see properly how visitors interact with our website to further strengthen it.
  • Security: It safeguards a user interface and, at times, acts as protection against various security threats.

 

Types of Cookies that are implemented

  • Essential Cookies- These have to ensure the proper functioning of a website.
  • Performance Cookies- They will enable us to track problems so that we can solve the performance bugs on this website.
  • Functionality Cookies- They enhance functionality and personalization.
  • Analytical Cookies- They help us in understanding visitor behavior, which, on the other hand will improve our ability in enhancing the experience for the users.
