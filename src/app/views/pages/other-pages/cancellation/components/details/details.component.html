Cancellation and Return Policy

At Fizoillu, we know plans change, and we want to be flexible enough to allow the smooth process of renting our dress products. For clarity purposes, please see the following Cancellation and Return Policy for cancellations, exchanges, and refunds.

 

  1. Order Cancellation

Customers can cancel their rental booking before the scheduled delivery or pickup date. To initiate a cancellation, please contact us at info@fizoillu.com with your order details. Timely communication helps us process your request efficiently.

 

  1. Exchange of Dresses

If the dress rented does not meet your expectations in terms of size, style, or otherwise, you can request an exchange. Exchanges are possible only if the product is available and must be done immediately after receiving the dress.

 

  1. Refund Procedure

Refunds are processed in respect of cancellations that have been made within the accepted period or in cases where the exchange cannot be carried out. Refunds shall not be processed in cash. For processing refunds, customers need to furnish their valid bank account details through info@fizoillu.com. Kindly ensure all information submitted is correct and error-free to avoid a delayed refund process.

 

  1. Condition for Return

Dresses shall be returned under the same conditions in which they have been received, with no stains, no damage, and without any changes. Failure to comply shall lead to the deduction from the refundable amount or refusal of a refund.

 

  1. Contact Us

For any queries related to cancellations, exchanges, and refunds, write to us at info@fizoillu.com.

By continuing with your booking, you agree to the terms as outlined in this Cancellation and Return Policy. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
